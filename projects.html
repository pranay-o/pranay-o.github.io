  • STM32 · Analog RF·LNA · Log Detector · LoRa·Hackathon 2026

    SkyWatch RF — Counter-Drone Detection Board

    A $61 RF detection board that passively detects drone control signals at 2.4 GHz and 5.8 GHz using an all-analog signal chain: PGA-103+ LNA, HMC1048 RF switch, dual bandpass filters, and an AD8318 log detector. WiFi false-alarms are rejected through bandwidth-based power density discrimination and an RC pulse-width filter — validated in LTSpice before hardware build. Alerts are relayed wirelessly via an RFM95W LoRa radio at 915 MHz, completely outside the monitored bands, with a theoretical detection range of ~950 m. PCB laid out on Rogers 4003C for stable RF performance at 5.8 GHz.
