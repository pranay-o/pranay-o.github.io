About

A bit about me.

I’m studying Engineering Physics at the University of British Columbia and currently work as a Hardware Operations Co-op at Hypercharge Networks — configuring Level 2 EVSE chargers, validating firmware deployments, and building internal tools that make charger setup faster.

On the design side, I’m a Battery Management System Firmware Engineer on UBC Formula Electric, the team that designs and builds a fully electric Formula race car. Before BMS I spent a year as the team’s Low Voltage Firmware Engineer working on the Rear Sensor Module.

On my own time, I like working on hardware projects and learning about control algorithms — like designing my own sensorless field-oriented BLDC motor controller from PCB to firmware.
